ABOUT

The core of why we created Cohort.work is creating a space to collaborate between teams and individuals who work together with shared interests and focused at design.

Free Resources

Design is an essence to creating a digital environment – and hence, it is extremely vital to create an ecosystem that empowers ideas, elevates thoughts and most importantly saves time.

At cohort.work, our vision is to –

Productivity Tools

Provide productivity tools that remove friction to the communication and collaboration process. The goal is to bridge the communication and asset sharing when it comes to design.

Design Kits

Carrying along with us the experience of more than two decades of creating digital solutions; provide rich design kits that serves a specific use cases for an industry. We fuel our years of design and engineering experience into creating business viable complete design solutions for app and web solutions.

Design Systems (coming soon)

Create a standardised platform and systems that act as a foundation for creating designs for various use cases. The aim is to deliver continuous improvement backed by a set of robust standards and atomic design system.
